## ðŸŽ¨ Photography Tips

### General Guidelines
- **Lighting**: Natural light is best, shoot near windows
- **Background**: Clean, simple backgrounds (white, wood, marble)
- **Angle**: Slight overhead angle (45Â°) works great for food
- **Props**: Add minimal props (napkin, coffee cup, herbs)
- **Focus**: Sharp focus on the main item, slight blur on background

### Food Styling Tips
- **Fresh is best**: Photograph items fresh from oven when possible
- **Show texture**: Capture crust, crumb, layers, and toppings
- **Cross sections**: For breads/cakes, show one sliced to reveal interior
- **Steam**: If possible, capture steam from fresh-baked items
- **Garnish**: Add fresh herbs, flour dusting, or fruit for color

### Quick Wins
1. **Use your smartphone** - Modern phones take excellent photos
2. **Golden hour** - Shoot in morning or late afternoon for best light
3. **Consistency** - Use similar lighting/styling across all photos
4. **Edit lightly** - Slightly boost contrast and saturation, but keep natural
5. **Compress** - Use tools like TinyPNG or Squoosh to optimize file sizes
